1 Add Protein

The most filling thing you can eat — it shouts to your gut hormones to stop. Double the dal, add paneer or egg.

2 Reduce Fat

9 calories a gram — twice anything else. Swap the free-pour glug for a spoon or a spray. Tastes like nothing, costs a fortune.

3 Add Fibre

The dial we sanded down on purpose. Hand-pounded rice, whole atta, a rotation of millets, skins left on. Fullness a steam mill stole back.

4 Reduce Simple Carbs

Not anti-carb — anti-spike. Smaller hill of white rice, no drinking your sugar. Re-proportion so grain is a part of the meal, not the meal.

Same dal-chawal you already love, re-proportioned. More protein, less fat, more fibre, fewer simple carbs — and keep every bit of the masala.
